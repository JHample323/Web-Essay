Antikythera Mechanism

Antikythera mechanism is an ancient greek analogue computer

The antikythera was the very first "computer", it was used in predicting astronomical positions. Being able to predict astronomical positions helped the Greeks when making calendars. The computer even had a four year cycle used for athletic games (Olympics). Without this device, the computer as we know it today could ultimately not exist. Now while this isn't a "digital" computer, it is considered an analog computer.

Modern Technology

Modern Technologies make life simpler, in a very short time, we have had technologies that have greatly benefited humans and the Earth. Some examples of modern technology would be the smartphone. We can talk to anyone whenever, wherever thanks to the smartphone, we can also access the internet using cellular providers. With technology like this we have a much simpler life, we can call for help if we are stuck, we can order food off our phone, listen to the music we want, we can even shop on our phone. Modern technology, even impacts education, at my school, we use iPad for everything and all the classes have smart boards. With things like these, we can watch videos to help us learn, we can get help from the teacher even at home, we can also do projects much easier.

Apple

One of the worlds biggest brands, started from the bottom.

Apple was created by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976. In 2015, Apple became the first brand to be valued over $700 billion. Apple started off only making computers but, in its later years moved towards more types of technology such as phones, tablets, mp3 players, and smart devices. Apple controls iTunes, the largest music retailer in the world. As of 2016, more than one billion apple products are in use. The first computer created by apple was handmade by Steve Wozniak. Apple’s second computer, the Apple II released in 1977.

Bill Gates

One of the richest men in the world and a genius.

Bill Gates was born October 28, 1955 in Seattle. He has a net worth of $90.4 billion and is among the richest men in the world. Ever since a kid, he has been very involved with computers. Bill was a co-founder of Microsoft, the leading company in technology. Gates and his wife are big time philanthropist and donate lots of money to charity. Gates and Warren Buffett created the Giving Pledge in which them and other billionaires pledge to give at least half their wealth to charity. Bill’s first program was an app in which you would play tic tac toe against the computer.

Pong

Pong is one of the very first video games. It is game in which is ball is hit back and forth using a joystick to control a paddle. If the user misses the ball then the computer or opponent earns a point. The goal of the game is to get a higher score than the opponent. Pong was manufactured by Atari and released in 1972. It was the first successful video, this established the video game industry. The success of this game inspired Atari to continue making original video games. Due to the impact of this game, the Smithsonian Institution has an original version of pong on display.
