Antikythera mechanism

Antikythera mechanism is an ancient greek analogue computer

Antikythera was the very first computer, it was used in predicting astronomical positions. Being able to predict astronomical postions helped the Greeks when making calanders. The computer even had a four year cycle used for athletic games (Olympics). Without this device, the computer as we know it today could ultimately not exist.
